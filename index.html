About our wonderful LemmiHoitajat


We're thrilled to have you here and showcase our passion for pet care in Helsinki.


With our well-trained team of pet caretakers, we provide top-notch services tailored to meet your furry friend's needs. Our ultimate goal? To pamper your pets so they miss you a little less while you're away!

Our team of highly trained pet caretakers is based in Helsinki, ready to provide top-notch care for your beloved pets. From regular walks and playtime to feeding and grooming, we offer a wide range of services to ensure that your pets miss you just a little bit less. With our professional care, you can rest assured knowing that your pets are in good hands. We strive to create a comfortable and safe environment for them, so that they can thrive and be happy in your absence. We believe that caring for pets is more than just a job - it's a passion.


Our Values


MISSION: Our mission is To pamper your pets so they miss you a little less while you're away!


VISION: Our vision at LemmiHoitajat, we understand the importance of your furry friends. Whether they're cats, dogs, or even hamsters, we treat them with love and care. No matter the size or breed, each pet is special to us.

HOW DOES THIS WORK?: You can contact us via phone call, text message, whatsapp message or email. Please specify your needs for care clearly from the starting day to end with details like if you need day care, walk, short visit, or overnight care at your or caretakers place, Also please provide your area (we will ask for exact address later). We will contact you with the proposed services detail as soon as possible to the phone number or email provided. Please remember, caretakers could charge extra if you need care within 24hrs of your first approach. We look forward to provide your pet the care that you have provided. We will send you forms to fill and sign before we begin our services. Best wishes
